Reva University is a private university, located in Kattigenahalli, Yelahanka, Bangalore. Established in 2012, the university is accredited by the NAAC with an 'A' Grade. Reva University currently offers 55 Undergraduate Programmes, 28 full-time & 2 Part-time Postgraduate Programmes, 17 Ph.D. Programmes in Engineering, Architecture, Science.
