OUR CERTICATES & ONLINE PROGRAMS

Reva University Bangalore was established under the Reva University Act and is recognised by UGC, the Bar Council of India, COA.Reva University Yelahanka is accredited by NBA and with B+ grade by NAAC. REVA University is ranked eighth in the State Wise categor by Open Magazine Best B-Schools Rankings 2023.


Reva University is a private university, located in Kattigenahalli, Yelahanka, Bangalore. Established in 2012, the university is accredited by the NAAC with an 'A' Grade. Reva University currently offers 55 Undergraduate Programmes, 28 full-time & 2 Part-time Postgraduate Programmes, 17 Ph.D. Programmes in Engineering, Architecture, Science and Technology, Commerce, Management Studies Law, Arts & Humanities, and Performing Arts. B.Tech is the most popular program of Reva University and admissions to the same are done on the basis of scores in REVA CET/KCET/COMED-K/Uni-GAUGE. B.Tech cutoff at Reva University ranges from 61443-149117 for the KCET exam. Check Reva University Admission 2023


Reva University provides excellent placement and internship opportunities to its students. Many top-notch companies from various sectors visit the campus every year to make placement offers. In the placement drive of 2022, a total of 3,512 job offers were made by participating companies. The highest package offered was INR 45 LPA. DELL, DHL, Philips, IBM, Berger, Amazon, Infosys, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Sunrise Systems, and Radcom Technologies are some of the top recruiters at Reva University.

About Us

Established in 2012, REVA University is a forerunner in delivering technology-based education. Recognised as one of the Top 50 private universities in India, REVA University,
with innovative pedagogy, exemplary infrastructure, and an industry-resilient curriculum that is designed for the future job market, strives to benchmark new milestones
in modern education. In less than two decades, the University has grown from a small community of 150 students to a vast institution that is educating and empowering 15,000+ students on campus.
